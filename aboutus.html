Who Are We

Global Audit is a modern audit delivery company – independent, reliable, and ready to meet the evolving demands of global compliance.

We operate through a growing network of experienced auditors and a digital-first mindset. Wherever audits are needed, we deliver – efficiently, impartially, and with integrity.

Where We Come From

Our journey began with hands-on experience in critical, high-compliance audits – including support for FM Approvals, regulatory inspections, and ISO-based management systems.

Over the past decade, we’ve built deep expertise in:

  • • Internal audits for ISO 9001, ISO/IEC 17020, and others
  • • Supplier audits across complex global supply chains
  • • Specialized audits for Ex equipment (ATEX, IECEx, FM, INMETRO, UKCA Ex, etc.)
  • • Supporting Certification Bodies with subcontracted audit delivery

Global Audit International, LLC is registered in Texas, with operations in North America and Latin America – and expanding.
