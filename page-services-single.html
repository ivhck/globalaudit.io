Core Services

At Global Audit, we provide independent, reliable, and efficient audit services across industries and geographies. Whether you need to ensure compliance, strengthen your quality systems, or meet client and regulatory requirements, our global network of expert auditors is ready to support you – wherever and whenever you need them.

Internal Audits

Ensure your management system is working – not just documented. We conduct audits aligned with ISO 9001 and other standards, identifying both nonconformities and opportunities for improvement. Ideal for organizations seeking certification readiness, gap analysis, or ongoing improvement.

Includes:

  • ISO 9001 Internal Audits
  • ISO/IEC 17020 Internal Audits
  • Customized Audit Programs
  • Remote or On-Site Execution

Supplier Audits

Trust your supply chain before it’s too late. We evaluate suppliers, contractors, and outsourced processes to help you reduce risk and ensure quality across the value chain. Global reach with local auditors.

Use cases:

  • Pre-qualification
  • Periodic evaluations
  • Critical supplier assessments
  • CSR / ESG / EHS / Quality audits

Ex Equipment & Regulatory Audits

Certified expertise in Ex and regulatory compliance. Our specialized auditors support Certification Bodies, Manufacturers, and Service Providers seeking ATEX, IECEx, INMETRO, UKCA Ex, and US NRTL compliance. We help you meet the requirements for ISO/IEC 80079-34 and beyond.

Applicable to:

  • Hazardous location equipment audits
  • FM/ATEX/IECEx readiness
  • Quality system (QAN/QAR) audits for notified body schemes

Audit Support for Certification Bodies

Expand your audit capacity with a trusted global partner. We support Certification Bodies (CBs) across product and management system certification schemes. Our qualified auditors act as your local eyes and ears, ensuring consistent, impartial, and technically competent audit delivery – under your brand or ours.

We support CBs with:

  • ISO/IEC 17020, 17021-1, 17065-based schemes
  • ATEX, IECEx, NRTL, INMETRO, UKCA Ex
  • Internal audits, witness audits, or subcontracted audits
  • Global coverage across the Americas, Europe, and Asia-Pacific

Custom Audit Programs

You define the need. We build the solution. From multi-site programs to multi-standards audits, we adapt to your operational context. Whether you operate in North America, LATAM, Europe, or Asia, we can deliver consistent, scalable audit solutions.
