Our Core Services: Reliability, Efficiency, Independence

At Global Audit, we provide independent, reliable, and efficient audit services across industries and geographies. Whether you need to ensure compliance, strengthen your quality systems, or meet client and regulatory requirements, our global network of expert auditors is ready to support you – wherever and whenever you need them.

Management System Audits

Ensure internal quality, environmental, and safety systems (e.g., ISO 9001) are working.

Supply Chain Verification

Pre-qualify and periodically assess suppliers, contractors, and outsourced processes.

Specialized & Regulatory

Expert audits for specialized schemes like ATEX, IECEx, INMETRO, and US NRTL compliance.

Internal Audits: Beyond Compliance

Ensure your management system is working – not just documented. We conduct audits aligned with ISO 9001 and other standards, identifying both nonconformities and **opportunities for improvement**. Ideal for organizations seeking certification readiness, gap analysis, or ongoing improvement.

Our Expertise Includes:

  • ISO 9001** and Integrated Management System Audits
  • ISO/IEC 17020** (Inspection Body) Audits
  • Gap Analysis & Certification Readiness Audits
  • Flexible Remote or On-Site Execution

Supplier Audits: **Mitigate Risk in Your Value Chain**

**Trust your supply chain before it’s too late.** We evaluate suppliers, contractors, and outsourced processes to help you **reduce risk** and ensure quality across the value chain. Global reach with local auditors ensures consistency and cultural awareness.

Common Use Cases:

  • Supplier **Pre-qualification** and Vetting
  • Periodic Performance & Quality Evaluations
  • Assessments covering **CSR, ESG, EHS, and Quality** standards
  • Critical Supplier Risk Assessments

Ex Equipment & Regulatory Audits: **Specialized Compliance**

**Certified expertise in Ex and regulatory compliance.** Our specialized auditors support Certification Bodies, Manufacturers, and Service Providers seeking **ATEX, IECEx, INMETRO, UKCA Ex, and US NRTL** compliance. We help you meet the requirements for ISO/IEC 80079-34 and beyond.

Applicable to:

  • Hazardous Location Equipment Audits
  • FM/ATEX/IECEx/NRTL **Readiness & Gap** Assessments
  • Quality System (QAN/QAR) Audits for Notified Body Schemes

Audit Support for Certification Bodies: **A Trusted Global Partner**

**Expand your audit capacity** with a trusted global partner. We support Certification Bodies (CBs) across product and management system certification schemes. Our qualified auditors act as your **local eyes and ears**, ensuring consistent, impartial, and technically competent audit delivery.

How We Support CBs:

  • Compliance with ISO/IEC **17020, 17021-1, 17065** standards
  • Specialized scheme support (ATEX, IECEx, NRTL, UKCA Ex)
  • Provision of Internal, Witness, or **Subcontracted Auditors**
  • **Global Coverage** across the Americas, Europe, and Asia-Pacific

Custom Audit Programs: **Your Need, Our Solution**

**You define the need. We build the solution.** From multi-site programs to multi-standards audits, we adapt to your operational context and unique compliance requirements. Whether you operate in North America, LATAM, Europe, or Asia, we can deliver consistent, **scalable** audit solutions tailored precisely to your internal protocols or client mandates.

Our custom programs often include bespoke checklists, integrated multi-standard reporting, and dedicated program management to ensure total consistency across your operations.
