What We Look For

We welcome professionals with experience in:

  • Management system audits (ISO 9001, 14001, 45001, 13485, AS9100, IATF 16949, ISO/IEC 17020, 80079-34, etc.)
  • Product certification audits (e.g., PED, MDD, ATEX, IECEx, US NRTL, INMETRO, UKCA Ex)
  • Supply chain, ESG, CSR, or second-party audits
  • Industry knowledge in manufacturing, energy, industrial safety, electronics, or regulated sectors

Minimum Requirements

  • Audit experience and relevant certifications (e.g., ISO Lead Auditor, ASQ CQA, Ex competent, etc.)
  • Impartiality and professionalism
  • Reliable internet access and basic tech literacy
